Web Security and Using the Internet Safely

The internet in itself is revolutionary and currently shapes much of our daily lives what with us now being in an age of information. From researching, talking with others across great distances, sharing media, working on the internet for jobs and other such uses the internet has effectiviely been integrated fully with our current way of life.

Due to how often we use the internet it is important to know how to use it safely as people fall victim to malicious activity almost every day online. Much of your activity is tracked online on most major websites and these websites often collect your data to compile in their databases. While they may not find and store things such as your name or where you live unless you allow them they still collect information such as places you frequent or watch online which is than used to personalize content you would further see online.
